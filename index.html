Tribute Production

"Where everyone is a star"

Tribute Production Studio is based in the University of Roehampton, in the Heart of London. We offer a full range of services such as recording, production, composition, mixing and mastering, concert & events organisation. Alongside this, the studio delivers external music workshops tailored to your organisation, and facilitates studio time and work experience for schools, young people and external organisations. The studio is passionate about making music accessible to everyone, particularly people who may have barriers to accessing music facilities. As well as qualifications and experience in the field of music, Staff at the Studio hold current DBS certificates and have training and experience in supporting people with a range of needs such as mental health, learning disabilities and Autism.
